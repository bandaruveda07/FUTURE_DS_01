# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Objective
Analyze online retail sales data to identify revenue trends,
top-selling products, customer behavior, and regional performance.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

## Dataset
Online Retail Dataset from Kaggle

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Top Countries
- Best Selling Products

## Key Insights
- Revenue trends vary across months.
- United Kingdom generated highest revenue.
- Few products contribute major sales.
- High-value customers drive significant business growth.

## Recommendations
- Improve customer retention strategies.
- Focus marketing on top-performing products.
- Expand sales in high-performing regions.
- Optimize inventory during peak sales periods.
